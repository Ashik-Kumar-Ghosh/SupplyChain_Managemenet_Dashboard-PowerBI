# 📊 Supply Chain Management Dashboard

## Overview
The **Supply Chain Management Dashboard** provides a consolidated view of supplier performance, costs, lead times, defect rates, and product distribution. It is designed to help supply chain managers, analysts, and decision-makers evaluate efficiency, identify bottlenecks, and optimize procurement strategies.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **Total Cost Analysis**  
  - ₹1.73M overall spend  
  - Daily and monthly order tracking  

- **Order Metrics**  
  - Ordered Quantity: 9,754 units  
  - Cost per unit range: ₹2.18 – ₹378.42  

- **Lead Time Insights**  
  - Avg Lead Time: 28.71 days  
  - Breakdown: Manufacturing (11.96 days), Supplier (16.75 days)  

- **Quality Monitoring**  
  - Avg Defect Rate: 3.09%  
  - SKU classification: High vs Low defect categories  

- **Supplier Comparison**  
  - Revenue, SKU counts, lead times, and product distribution  
  - Location-wise cost breakdown across Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Kolkata, and Mumbai
